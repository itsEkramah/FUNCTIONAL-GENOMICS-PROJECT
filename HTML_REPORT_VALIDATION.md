# PathoScope AI HTML Report System Validation Report

This report documents the design, verification, and formatting of the redesigned static HTML pipeline report generators for both genome and transcriptomic analysis workflows.

## 1. Unified Design Language
The static reports are compiled as stand-alone HTML files. They share a consistent, premium dark-mode styling scheme that is highly legible and aligns with modern design practices:
- **Palette**: Slate and deep space backgrounds (`#0d1117`, `#161b22`) matched with cohesive highlights:
  - Primary text and accents: Deep blue (`#58a6ff`) and orange (`#f0883e`)
  - GSEApy/ORA confidence indicators: green, amber, and red badges depending on level (`HIGH`, `MEDIUM`, `LOW`).
- **Typography**: Uses clean, highly readable font family stack (`Outfit`, `Inter`, `sans-serif`) imported dynamically.

---

## 2. FASTA/FASTQ Annotation Reports
Generated by `HtmlReport` in [html_report.py](file:///g:/LAST%20FINAL%20FUNCTIONAL%20GENOICS%20PROJECT/FUNCTIONAL%20GENOMICS%20PROJECT/backend/reports/html_report.py):
- **Metrics Summary**: Renders Sequence QC metrics (length, GC%, N count), classification (NCBI Taxon ID, scientific name, full lineage), and counts of identified ORF, Pfam, and SwissProt annotations.
- **AI Pathobiology Summary**: Focuses on evidence-grounded AI pathogenesis findings, literature abstracts, and limitations.
- **Dynamic Visualization Grid**: Inspects the jobs visualizations folder and dynamically embeds all quality control and genomic charts in a responsive grid.
- **Annotated Tables**: Tabulates comprehensive homologous proteins, predicted domains, and mapped KEGG metabolic pathway lists.

## 3. DEG Analysis Reports
Generated by `compile_deg_reports` in [workflow_deg.py](file:///g:/LAST%20FINAL%20FUNCTIONAL%20GENOICS%20PROJECT/FUNCTIONAL%20GENOMICS%20PROJECT/backend/pipeline/workflow_deg.py):
- **Metrics Summary**: Outlines dataset normalization metrics, ID mapping ratios, and regulation classification (counts of Upregulated, Downregulated, and Non-significant genes).
- **AI Pathobiology Summary**: Details GSEA and ORA transcription interpretations and literature context.
- **Dynamic Visualization Grid**: Automatically detects and displays all 12 differential expression plots (including Volcano plots, MA plots, DEG distribution histograms, top upregulated/downregulated gene bars, GO/KEGG dot and bar plots).
- **Annotated Tables**: Tabulates top enriched GO biological processes/functions, top enriched KEGG biochemical pathways, and hyperlinked NCBI PubMed reference bibliography cards.
